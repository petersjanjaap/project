Global Britain

Global Britain is the British Cabinet's slogan for its future trade policy.
Yet how Global is Britain now? And what are the opportunities after Brexit?

This project shows the development of Britain as a trade nation over the years 2000-2017,
and displays its trade partners, the composition of trade flows and home production of these partners.
